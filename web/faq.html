Frequently Asked Questions

HomeFAQ
How does InstaTalk work?

InstaTalk uses advanced AI technology to provide real-time translation for text and voice communication.

Is InstaTalk available for both Android and iOS devices?

Yes, InstaTalk is compatible with both Android and iOS platforms.

How many languages does InstaTalk support?

InstaTalk supports translation in over 180 languages, ensuring comprehensive language coverage.

Can I use InstaTalk offline?

Unfortunately, InstaTalk requires an internet connection for real-time translation.

Is InstaTalk free to use?

InstaTalk offers both free and premium versions with additional features. The free version includes basic translation capabilities.

How do I switch between languages in InstaTalk?

InstaTalk can automatically detect the language being used, but you can also manually select languages if needed.

Is my data secure with InstaTalk?

InstaTalk takes user privacy seriously and employs robust security measures to protect your data.

Can I translate voice messages with InstaTalk?

Yes, InstaTalk allows you to translate voice recordings, making it easy to understand spoken content in different languages.

How can I get early access to InstaTalk updates and features?

You can subscribe with your email to receive notifications about app launches and updates.
